# Synent-Technology-Task5-Sales-Data-Analysis
## Overview

This project focuses on performing Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Superstore Sales Dataset using Python. The objective is to uncover meaningful business insights, identify sales and profit trends, evaluate product performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis includes data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, and business intelligence reporting using popular Python libraries.

---

## Project Objectives

* Understand the structure and quality of the dataset.
* Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
* Analyze sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Study customer segments and regional sales patterns.
* Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail sales transaction records, including:

* Order Details
* Customer Information
* Product Categories
* Sales
* Profit
* Quantity
* Discounts
* Shipping Details
* Regional Information

### Dataset Size

* Records: 9,994
* Columns: 21

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection

The Superstore dataset was imported into Jupyter Notebook for analysis.

### 2. Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Removed duplicate records
* Converted date columns into proper datetime format

### 3. Feature Engineering

Created additional columns such as:

* Year
* Month
* Month Name
* Month-Year

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis

Performed analysis on:

* Sales Trends
* Profit Trends
* Product Performance
* Category Performance
* Customer Segments
* Regional Sales
* Discount Impact

### 5. Visualization

Created charts and graphs to understand:

* Monthly Sales Trends
* Monthly Profit Trends
* Category-wise Sales
* State-wise Sales
* Discount vs Profit Relationships

---

## Key Findings

* Total Sales exceeded **$2.29 Million**
* Total Profit exceeded **$286K**
* Technology was the most profitable category.
* Copiers generated the highest profit among sub-categories.
* Consumer customers contributed the highest share of sales.
* Higher discounts often resulted in lower profitability.
* Sales peaked during year-end periods.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing technology products.
* Promote profitable product categories through targeted marketing.
* Optimize discount strategies to improve profit margins.
* Strengthen customer retention programs.
* Utilize historical sales data for demand forecasting.
